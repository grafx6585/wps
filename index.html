Requirements to Rent

  • Have Your Personal Watercraft License
  • Pay a damage deposit - Refundable after the ski is returned.
  • Be over 18 years of age.
  • Sign a liability form.

Note: Rental rates do not include fuel.

Discounts: Law enforcement and military personnel discounts available.

FAQ about our rentals

Yes, per State of Connecticut, you need to have a valid personal watercraft license to rent a jet ski.

Yes, a refundable damage deposit is required when renting. It will be refunded after the ski is returned in good condition.

Yes, you must sign a liability form before renting.
